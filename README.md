**Pawfect** is an interactive web app for swiping and liking adorable cats. It combines **swipe gestures** with **intuitive buttons**, designed for **both desktop and mobile** for smooth, enjoyable use.  

---

## How to Use (Desktop & Mobile)

**Desktop:**
1. Swipe cats **right** to like, **left** to dislike using your mouse or trackpad.  
2. Click the **Like**, **Dislike**, or **Back** buttons below the cat for quick actions.  
3. View your liked cats in the **Liked Cats Summary** grid below the main deck.  
4. Click any cat to **enlarge**, or use the **`+N` overlay** to see the full album.

**Mobile:**
1. Swipe cats **right** or **left** with your finger.  
2. Buttons are **stacked vertically on the right** for easy thumb access.  
3. Undo a swipe using the **Back button**.  
4. Tap any liked cat in the summary or album to **view it larger**.  

> Tip: The app automatically adjusts layout, button size, and interactions for **any screen size**, so swiping and liking is smooth whether you‚Äôre on desktop or mobile.

## Features

### 1. Cat Deck & Swipe
- Swipe with smooth **drag-and-rotate animation** for realistic interaction.  
- **Preloads next cats** to ensure seamless swiping.  
- Undo your last action with the **Back button**.  

### 2. Interactive Buttons
- **Desktop:** Buttons are centered horizontally for easy clicking.  
- **Mobile:** Buttons are stacked vertically on the right for thumb-friendly interaction.  
- Buttons scale and highlight on hover/click for clear feedback.  

### 3. Liked Cats Summary & Album
- Shows **count of liked cats** and a **grid of liked images**.  
- Click any cat to **view it enlarged**.  
- Use the **`+N` overlay** to access the **full liked album**, with clickable images for enlarging.  

### 4. Responsive & Cross-Device
- Fully responsive: works on **desktop, tablet, and mobile**.  
- Layout and card sizes **adjust automatically** to fit any screen.  
- Optimized for **pointer and touch input**.  

### 5. ‚ÄúNo More Cats‚Äù Popup
- Appears when all cats in the deck have been swiped.  
- Options: **refresh the deck** for new cats, or **close** to view only your liked summary.  

### 6. Performance
- Fetches random cat images from [Cataas API](https://cataas.com).  
- **Parallel fetching** ensures fast loading and smooth swipe animations.
